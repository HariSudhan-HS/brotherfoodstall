🍽️ How Our Food Ordering Website Works
Whether you're hungry for a quick snack or a full meal, our platform makes it easy to order food online in just a few clicks.

✅ 1. Browse the Menu
Explore a wide range of cuisines and dishes from local restaurants and cloud kitchens. Categories like Starters, Main Course, Beverages, and Desserts help you find what you're craving.

✅ Filter dishes by cuisine, dietary preference (veg/non-veg), and popularity.

✅ 2. Login with Google (Secure & Fast)
Sign in securely using your Google account. No need to remember passwords — just one click and you're in.

🔒 We use OAuth 2.0 for authentication, ensuring your personal data stays safe.

✅ 3. Add to Cart
Found what you like? Click “Add to Cart” and keep browsing. You can adjust quantities or remove items anytime before checkout.

🛒 See a live cart summary with total price and estimated delivery time.

✅ 4. Checkout & Pay
Once you’re ready, head to the checkout. Choose from payment options like:

UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe)

Credit/Debit Cards

Net Banking

Cash on Delivery (COD)

💳 Fast and secure transactions with payment gateway integration.

✅ 5. Track Your Order
After placing your order, track its progress live — from kitchen preparation to the moment it’s on the way to your door.

📍 Real-time GPS tracking of the delivery agent.

✅ 6. Enjoy Your Meal!
Sit back and relax — your hot, fresh food is on the way! Don’t forget to rate your experience and leave feedback.

⭐ Your feedback helps us improve our service and food quality.
